# 💰 Monetization Strategy - XHS Video Downloader

**Available Payment Methods:** Stripe, Alipay, WeChat Pay
**Target Market:** Chinese users (XHS is primarily Chinese)
**Recommended Strategy:** Freemium + WeChat/Alipay

---

## 🎯 Best Monetization Model for Your Market

### Recommended: Freemium + Multi-Payment

**Free Tier:**
- 5 downloads/day
- 480p max resolution
- No transcripts
- Community support

**Premium Tier (¥35/month or $4.99):**
- Unlimited downloads
- 1080p resolution
- AI transcripts
- Batch download (5 videos)
- Email support

**Pay-Per-Use ($0.99 for 10 downloads):**
- One-time purchase
- 720p resolution
- No expiration

## 💳 Payment Priority

### Priority 1: WeChat Pay ⭐⭐⭐
- 90% of Chinese users have WeChat

- Instant confirmation

### Priority 2: Alipay ⭐⭐⭐
- 80% of Chinese users have Alipay
- Lowest fees: 0.55%

- High trust

### Priority 3: Stripe ⭐⭐
- International users
- Higher fees: 2.9% + $0.30

- Already partially implemented
