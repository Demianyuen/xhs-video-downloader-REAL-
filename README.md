## 📊 Project Overview

XHS Video Downloader is a free, fast, and easy-to-use web application for downloading videos from Xiaohongshu (Little Red Book) in multiple resolutions without registration or installation.

### Key Features
- ✅ Free forever (no hidden fees)
- ✅ Multiple resolutions (1080p, 720p, 480p, 360p)
- ✅ No registration required
- ✅ No installation needed
- ✅ Works on all devices
- ✅ Fast download speeds
- ✅ 100% safe and secure

## 💰 Monetization Strategy

### Recommended Model: Freemium + Multi-Payment

**Free Tier:**
- 5 downloads/day
- 480p max resolution
- No transcripts
- Community support

**Premium Tier (¥35/month or $4.99):**
- Unlimited downloads
- 1080p resolution
- AI transcripts
- Batch download (5 videos)
- Email support

**Pay-Per-Use ($0.99 for 10 downloads):**
- One-time purchase
- 720p resolution
- No expiration

### Payment Methods (Priority)
1. **WeChat Pay** (0.6% fee) - 90% of Chinese users
2. **Alipay** (0.55% fee) - 80% of Chinese users
3. **Stripe** (2.9% + $0.30) - International users

### Revenue Projections
- Month 1: $50-165
- Month 3: $275-825
- Month 6: $1,100-3,300
